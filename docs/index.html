What Is This?

BKRLab Space Weather Alerts sends SMS notifications when the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) Space Weather Prediction Center issues significant geomagnetic, solar radiation, or radio blackout alerts.

Alerts are sourced directly from NOAA's public data feeds. Messages are sent only when real events meet threshold criteria — there are no promotional messages and no third-party data sharing.

Alert Types

  • Equipment Satellite drag, GPS accuracy degradation, HF radio blackouts, power grid impacts (G2+ and S2+ events)
  • Wildlife Geomagnetic disruption affecting migratory bird navigation, cetacean magnetoreception, and sea turtle homing (Kp ≥ 5)
  • HSP Advisory Epidemiological correlation advisories for highly sensitive persons — sudden storm commencements and sustained geomagnetic elevation (Kp ≥ 5)

How to Subscribe

Text START to +1 (978) 483-0684 from your mobile phone. You'll receive a one-time confirmation message. After that, you'll receive alerts only when significant space weather events occur.

How to Unsubscribe

Text STOP to +1 (978) 483-0684 at any time — you'll be removed immediately and automatically.

You can also use the web form to unsubscribe if you prefer not to send a text.

Message Frequency & Cost

Messages are sent only when significant space weather events occur. During quiet conditions this may be zero messages per week; during active periods it may be several per day. Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier.
